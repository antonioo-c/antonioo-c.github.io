Results

Our method significantly outperforms existing driving world models in both visual quality and trajectory following accuracy:

  • Achieves better scores on LPIPS (0.2577), PSNR (22.1746), SSIM (0.6569), FID (6.3689), and FVD (69.2641)
  • Demonstrates superior trajectory following with a trajectory error of 0.00027
  • Enables advanced applications like corner case synthesis, scene editing, and VLA planning assistance
