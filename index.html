Biography

I am currently a master student at Peking University, supervised by Dr. Shanghang Zhang. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University in 2023. I had the pleasure to visit Tel Aviv University working with Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or in 2025, and have remotely collaborated with Prof. Kurt Keutzer from UC Berkeley in 2024.

My research interest lies in Generative Models and Representation Learning. Currently I am woking on improving controllability of generative models.

I actively participate in InstantX, an organization that open-sources generative models. The projects I am part of have received over 15,000 stars on GitHub.
